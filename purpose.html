Our Trade Model

Ensuring Compliance, Traceability & Ethical Sourcing.

  1. SOURCING: Purchase from verified small-scale producers.
  2. VALIDATION: Compliance due diligence; fire assay & paperwork verification.
  3. LOGISTICS: Secured & insured transport to refinery.
  4. REFINING: Accredited facilities, quality control.
  5. DELIVERY: Responsible sale with full traceability.

Cycle: 6–8 working days • Fully insured • OECD-aligned.

Core Functions

  • Responsible Sourcing & Verification: titles, KYS, on-site checks, assays.
  • Due Diligence & Risk Management: OECD 5-step, AML/CFT, continuous monitoring.
  • Secure Logistics: Armored transport, chain-of-custody, customs, insurance.
  • Responsible Offtake: Accredited refiners, authorized end-users, audit trail.
