Who we are

Helvetica Commodities is a Compliance-Driven Platform for Responsible Precious-Metals Supply Chains.

Helvetica Resources is an independent commodity firm operating at the intersection of responsible mineral sourcing, due diligence, and export compliance. We work with artisanal and small-scale mining (ASM) communities to integrate their production into fully legal, traceable, and OECD-aligned supply chains.

  • Ensure the lawful origin of precious metals
  • Strengthen transparency and documentation
  • Support ASM formalisation and regulatory integration
  • Implement AML/CFT controls consistent with international standards
  • Facilitate secure, verified export pathways

Qué hacemos

Ensuring Compliance, Traceability & Ethical Sourcing.

Our Trade Model

  1. SOURCING: Purchase from verified small-scale producers.
  2. VALIDATION: Compliance due diligence; Fire assay & paperwork verification.
  3. LOGISTICS: Efficient, secured, and insured transport to refinery.
  4. REFINING: Processing and quality control by accredited facilities.
  5. DELIVERY: Responsible sale to authorized end-users with full traceability.

Transaction cycle: 6–8 working days • Fully insured and traceable • Aligned with OECD Responsible Supply Chain Standards.

Core Functions

  • Responsible Sourcing & Verification: Legal titles, KYS, on-site checks, fire assay, third-party provenance.
  • Due Diligence & Risk Management: OECD 5-step, AML/CFT, audits, continuous monitoring.
  • Secure, Insured Logistics: Transporte blindado, chain-of-custody, aduanas, seguro.
  • Refining & Responsible Offtake: Refinadores acreditados, venta a end-users autorizados, audit trail.

Sostenibilidad y Comercio Responsable

FOSTERING TRANSPARENT AND INTEGRATED SUPPLY CHAINS

Responsible Trade

  • Follows the OECD Guidelines for Responsible Mineral Supply Chains, integrating ASM producers into legal trade.
  • Risk-based due diligence: identify, assess, and manage risks in sourcing.
  • Promotes formalization: supports ASM communities instead of excluding them.
  • Prevents harmful practices: zero tolerance for forced or child labor and environmental abuse.
  • Supports development: ensures that responsible trade improves local livelihoods.

Our Sustainability Commitments

  • Human Rights & Labor Standards: No child labor; No forced or exploitative labor; safe working conditions.
  • Environmental Responsibility: Oversight of chemical use; improved practices; safer extraction methods.
  • Support for ASM Communities: Engagement, registro legal y cumplimiento; desarrollo local.
  • Governance & Regulatory Alignment: OECD-aligned due diligence; LBMA conflict-free; AML/CFT en cada etapa.
